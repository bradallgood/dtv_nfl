Where can I watch 2024 NFL football games?

You can watch NFL football games on the following TV channels: CBS, NBC, FOX ESPN, TNT, NFL Network & NFL Sunday Ticket.

Does DIRECTV still have NFL Sunday Ticket?

DIRECTV continues to offer the NFL Sunday Ticket to our DIRECTV commercial establishments nationwide. DIRECTV business customers interested in ordering the NFL Sunday Ticket may do so now by calling 877-245-2183. DIRECTV offers football fans an exclusive NFL experience by integrating favorite apps like YouTube and Prime Video. 

What package includes NFL RedZone?

NFL RedZone is included in DIRECTV SPORTS PACK for $14.99 per month and is NFL RedZone is automatically included in the PREMIER TV package.
